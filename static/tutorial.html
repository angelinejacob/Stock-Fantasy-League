Tutorial

To be more familiar with our system, we suggest the following materials.

Introduction

We aim to provide a tutorial that caters to various audiences: beginners learning about stock trading and markets and to those already familiar with trading and intending to use our application. We intend to have a fully working visual, interactive tutorial on our website that walks through the various steps in stock trading; in particular, the interactive tutorial will have sample stock data that can be inspected as if it were real data seen in-game/league. Furthermore, we also plan to provide a second tutorial describing the process of how to use the application and its functionalities. As of right now we have outlined the different components that users will see in the final tutorial. 

The tutorial about stock trading itself is intended to be interactive, while the one describing usage of the application may either be interactive or a set of instructions with clearly labeled pictures presented in an easy-to-read format.

Upon clicking on the button to proceed, the text page will be replaced by sample stock market data for some company. The contents of this data include but are not limited to: Company Name, Current Price, Volume, and other information. Furthermore, a graphical representation of the stock data of that company over the past week will be provided on the left of the page; users can specify the range of stock data values to be examined which will update the graph and stock data. 

Should the user be interested in learning about how to use our application, the button/link to “Functionalities of the Application” should be clicked. This will generate a page of text/pictures describing the functionalities of our application, including how to manage one’s profile/portfolio, how to join/create a league, how to interpret leaderboard information, and other functions provided on the website.

The RSS feed will distribute a list of headlines related to Stock Fantasy League and update notices to all the users of Stock Market League in an organized, easy-to-read fashion. As site administrators, we will frequently post announcements, challenges, and new site features on the front page of the Stock Fantasy League website as well as this page.

The Contact form for the user to send us any tips, suggestions, bugs, or any concerns. It will be placed at the bottom of each page. We welcome any feedback from our users. Your suggestions will be our motivation towards this project.
